# Busy Bee

When you finish your tramp award by singing with everyone, you can begin trying for your Busy Bee award by
singing with everyone a second time. When you have achieved Tramp or Super Tramp status, put a small message under the
tramp card that says "Sing with everyone again to be a Busy Bee!"

The singer cards should revert to their lighter color but the checkmarks should remain present. Now when you check someone off it goes dark
again and a second checkmark gets added.

If you sing with everyone in the other three parts then yourself a second time, another card should appear under the tramp card that says
"You're a busy bee!"

There should be a checkbox on the event configuration on the admin page to allow busy bee. This should all be done only if it is checked.
